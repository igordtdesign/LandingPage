About me:

I'm Igor Dalmolin

A 27 year old UX/UI designer from southern Brazil. My career made it possible for me to travel around this huge country and inspire me along my journey.

While still in college (UFSM), I started working as an intern Graphic Designer and Production Assistant. And then, Covid-19 hit and I had to find new ways to keep working and improving my skills.

Craving to improve, I took courses in art, design and music, and started working as a Digital Designer for a tech startup. That’s where I found out about UX/UI Design, I fell in love… Still needing to support myself and my cat, I was offered a job as an Art Director, which made it possible for me to pursue a postgraduate degree in UX Design at PUCRS.

I am thankful for all of this journey, but I am not done yet. I am always striving to learn more, and I am currently learning how to code.
