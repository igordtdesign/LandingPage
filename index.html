About me

I'm Igor Dalmolin.

Product Designer from southern Brazil, with a background in graphic design and art direction. Over 6 years working across visual identity, campaigns, social media strategy, and UX - always at the intersection of creativity and technology.

I started as a Graphic Designer and Production Assistant while still in college. When Covid hit, I had to adapt fast - and discovered that the problem-solving mindset was already there.

I moved into tech, learned UX, and completed a postgraduate degree at PUCRS with a perfect grade. Today I work as Art Director at Agência CENTRO, leading visual systems for public institutions - while building product skills and learning to code.

My edge isn't just the craft. It's the ability to direct: to know what to build, why it matters, and how to communicate it clearly - with or without AI in the room.

Available for full-time, part-time or freelance.
